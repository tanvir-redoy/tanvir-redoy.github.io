--- title: "AI-System Co-Design Framework" excerpt: "Development of frameworks that jointly optimize artificial intelligence models and system architectures for improved performance and scalability." collection: portfolio publication_venue: "MobiSys 2024" tags: - AI - Systems - Co-design ---

This work presents a novel approach to system design by considering both AI model requirements and system constraints. Through AI-system co-design principles, we demonstrate significant improvements in resource utilization and end-to-end performance across multiple application domains.
